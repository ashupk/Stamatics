Informal Session with Mr Balakrishna Shetty


About the Speaker


Mr Shetty completed a B.Sc Honours in Mathematics at Presidency College, Calcutta (1970) and an M.Sc. (Mathematics) at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur (1972). After a period of research in Mathematics at the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai, he joined the Indian Statistical Service in 1973 and was Assistant Director, dealing with Industries and Trade Statistics in Central Statistical Organization, Government of India, New Delhi. In 1976, he joined the Indian Foreign Service.
Currently, Guest Faculty, Mathematics Department at Indraprastha Institute for Information Technology, Delhi(IIITD). Resource Person for Nation-wide Sensitization Programme on Mathematics for Teachers, Vigyan Prasar, Dept of Science & Technology, Govt. of India
Author of What is Mathematics? published by National Book Trust India, 2013

Prof. Krishnan did his integrated MSc at IIT Kanpur. He had joined Infosys Tech after his degree and worked there for 7 years before deciding to work in Number Theory. He had finished his PhD from IMSc, Chennai and currently a Professor at Shiv Nadar University.
