Informal Sessions

Informal sessions are organised for the students of our department for a healthy interaction with seniors and other members.
Time to time we organise sessions for effective healthy growth and to make the learning environment.
We have various informal sessions to build the bonding amongst the members of department . Experience matters a lot in curriculum and thus interaction between seniors and juniors is promoted. We have sessions for Internships, Placements. At the time of pre-registration we have sessions for seniors and juniors to help in choosing the subjects.
Stamatics also conducts mentoring sessions with the help of MTH academic mentors primarily for the first year students.
