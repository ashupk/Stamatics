Statistics Lectures

We organise the Stamatics Group lecture series taken by the professors of our institute,we also had faculty from ISI ,IISC.We also invite professors of other departments for Stamatics lecture.

The first stamatics talk of session was by Prof. Shobha Madan on Oct 17th. The title of the talk was: Vandermonde Determinant, Uncertainty and Signal Recovery.

We also had Mahan Maharaj( recipient of Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Award in Mathematical Sciences) giving lecture on :"INTRODUCTION TO HYPERBOLIC GEOMETRY".

The students working on different projects in field of mathematics are also encouraged to present their work through the lectures addressed by them.
