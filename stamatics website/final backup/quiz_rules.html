Mathemania Rules

"MATHEMANIA" is the quiz competition organized by STAMATICS .It is team based event with each team consisting of 3 members.The questions are prepared keeping in mind the 1st year students and PG students so that no one has advantage over others. We will have a series of quiz this semester.Apart from the goodies to winning team of a quiz ,each individual members of winning team gets 5 point and goodies,similarly the individual of team who came second will get 3 points and similarly third will get 2 points each.At the end of semester the individuals with maximum points will get certificate and prize.Note that points is for each individual of team not for the team so that can take part in all quiz and not bounded to be in same team
